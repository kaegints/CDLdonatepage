Why your gift matters

Help students with Learning Disabilities learn, connect, and thrive.

Centre for Diverse Learners provides academic and social programming for children, youth, and adults with Learning Disabilities and their families across Halton and Hamilton.

CDL is a registered charitable organization and does not receive government funding for its initiatives. Donations help keep programs accessible and low-cost while supporting continuity between funding cycles.

800+total learners served in 2024–25
1,020+served through advocacy, resource calls, and free workshops
20+workshops, conference sessions, and panels hosted

Your support helps learners build literacy, math, executive functioning, social confidence, and classroom readiness through programs designed for their strengths, challenges, and learning needs.

Academic growth Reading, math, homework-health, and individualized learning support.
Social confidence Programs that support friendships, emotional regulation, and belonging.
Family advocacy Guidance and workshops that help parents navigate learning needs.

Monthly donors help CDL plan ahead.

Consistent giving gives CDL a more reliable base of support for programs, materials, workshops, and learner services between funding cycles.

  • Steady program support: help CDL maintain low-cost academic and social programming throughout the school year.
  • Simple monthly giving: choose an amount that fits your budget and adjust it if your circumstances change.
  • Clear donor records: receive charitable tax receipt information for eligible donations through the donation platform.
